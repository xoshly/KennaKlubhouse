Covid-19 Procedures

We are fully commited to keep your child and our staff safe as possible. Here are some of our Covid-19 policies:
  • All staff and children attending must be fully vaccinated
  • If child is too young for vaccine, Covid test must be negative 48 hours prior to attending
  • Face masks are required while in the building
  • Staff and children must wash hands when entering and prior exiting the building and prior snack time
  • For our full policy list, visit CDC Guidelines.
